# COHERENCE-26_NODEtorious - **SCOUT**

A corporate-oriented, highly scalable platform designed to streamline lead management, automate workflows, and enhance user engagement through advanced Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning integrations.

## Core Features

### Workflow & Automation
*   **Custom Workflows**: Build tailored processes to suit specific business needs.
*   **Collaborative Builder**: Work together with your team in real-time.
*   **Drag & Drop Interface**: Intuitive design for rapid workflow creation.
*   **Editable Workflows**: Easily modify and adapt workflows on the fly.
*   **Dynamic Data Collection**: Gather and process data automatically.

### AI & Machine Learning Integrations
*   **Lead Scoring (ML)**: Intelligent evaluation of leads to prioritize conversion efforts.
*   **Time Personalization**: Optimized timing for interactions, driven by machine learning.
*   **Gemini Integration**: Advanced, multifaceted AI capabilities.
*   **Custom Mail Generation**: Context-aware email drafting powered by Llama 3.5 AI.
*   **Data Analysis Chatbot**: An interactive assistant designed to help, guide, and go through your data.

### Communication & Interactions
*   **Automated Email Operations**: Seamless automated sending and receiving utilizing Nodemailer and IMAP.
*   **Telegram Bot**: On-the-go control and system management.
*   **Call Functionality**: Providing offline access to essential system capabilities.
*   **Platform Extension**: Effortlessly capture and drop leads directly into the platform.
*   **Humanlike Behavior**: Engineered with Redis to simulate human-like interaction patterns and behavior.

### Architecture & Technology Stack
Built with scalable technology to ensure high performance, security, and enterprise-grade reliability.
*   **Authentication**: Secure access and identity verification utilizing JWT and Google Auth.
*   **Database**: Robust and flexible data storage utilizing MongoDB.
*   **Data Ingestion**: Full support for comprehensive CSV and Excel file inputs.
